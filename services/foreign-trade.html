Foreign Trade Services (EXIM)

We are dedicated to genuinely assisting our clients in Export and Import (EXIM) consultancy. Our team possesses strong expertise in trade regulations, documentation, and incentive structures under India’s Foreign Trade Policy.

We provide swift and accurate solutions, ensuring compliance through electronic documentation and filings to prevent port delays and maximize incentive benefits.

Our EXIM Advisory Services

  • EPCG Scheme: Import of capital goods without customs duty for production activities.
  • SEIS: Incentives for service exporters under the Service Export from India Scheme.
  • Advance Authorizations: Duty-free import of inputs used in export products.
  • RODTEP: Refund of embedded duties and taxes on export products.
  • RoSCTL: Rebate of State and Central Taxes for export of apparels and made-ups.
  • Star Export House: Special benefits based on export performance of the company.

Our EXIM specialists ensure all legal requirements are fulfilled efficiently, helping exporters focus on growth while we manage compliance and incentives.
